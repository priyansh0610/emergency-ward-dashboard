Staffing and beds notes

This page helps the team distinguish between flow pressure, staffing shortages, and bed capacity constraints.

  • Tables are intentionally concrete because they are trusted quickly in academic demonstration settings.
  • Average bed turnaround time is calculated as Σ bed turnaround times ÷ number of completed bed turnaround events.
  • The visual balance mirrors the staffing and beds wireframe but is upgraded into a cleaner presentable prototype.
