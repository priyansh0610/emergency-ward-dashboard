Risk score explanation

The score reduces reliance on colour only.

Risk score = acuity score + waiting time score + alert score + boarding score

Red and orange patients receive higher base scores. Waiting time, active alerts, and boarding status increase the score.

Prototype limitations

Important to keep the academic submission realistic.

  • This is a static educational prototype and not connected to a hospital system.
  • All patient records, alerts, incidents, and staffing values are fictional demo data.
  • The risk score is a design feature for demonstration, not a validated clinical risk tool.
  • A real implementation would require information governance, clinical safety review, security testing, and integration with hospital systems.

Educational prototype only. Fictional data. Not for real patient care.
